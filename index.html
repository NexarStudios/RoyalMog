About RoyalMog

RoyalMog is a community-driven popularity poll for TikTok royals — the princes and princesses who rule the For You Page. Every vote counts, the rankings update live, and the crown changes hands at any moment.

How It Works
I
Browse the Royals
Head to the Cast a Vote tab and scroll through all the princes and princesses. Tap any card to visit their TikTok profile first if you need a reminder of their reign.
II
Cast Your Vote
Select the royal you believe deserves the crown and hit the vote button. Your vote is recorded instantly — no accounts, no sign-ups.
III
One Vote Every 24 Hours
To keep things fair, each device can vote once per day. After voting, a countdown shows exactly when you can cast your next vote.
IV
Watch the Live Rankings
The Live Rankings tab updates in real time. The top three royals earn a spot on the podium — first place takes the golden crown.
